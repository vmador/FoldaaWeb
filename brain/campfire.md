# The Campfire Ecosystem

Campfire is the hearth of Foldaa, where creators and clients converge to build the future of software through specialized units called **Temps**.

## Conceptual Lifecycle

### 1. System → Temp Creation
The System creates **Temps** according to market demand or specific client needs. Each Temp is a "pre-configured mini-Tent" that includes its own stack, infrastructure, and product templates.

### 2. Client → Renting a Temp
The client "ignites" a project by renting a Temp. They do not hire individuals; they rent a system.
- **Duration**: How long the Temp remains active (e.g., 4 weeks).
- **Capacity**: How many **Builders** contribute simultaneously (e.g., 3 builders).
- **Level**: Specialized tiers (Junior, Senior, AI-heavy).

### 3. Builders → Execution
Builders enter the Temp to execute the **Source of Truth**. They are not project owners but temporary contributors. Matching is automated based on skills, reputation, and experience.

### 4. Transferability & The Brain
If a Builder leaves, another can step in seamlessly. The Temp is a self-contained environment:
- **Brain**: Keeps the history of decisions and roadmap context.
- **Assets**: Living designs, code, and tests.
- **Guidelines**: Clear architecture and design systems.

## Source of Truth (Standard Manifest)
Every Temp must have a manifest of 4 Markdown files to ensure universal context:
- `system.md`: Architecture, data models, and logic.
- `design.md`: Aesthetic guidelines and UI components.
- `roadmap.md`: Deliverables, weeks, and milestones.
- `stack.md`: Technical specifications and environment config.

## Business Model & Monetization
- **Renta de Temps**: Fees based on time + capacity.
- **Premium Temps**: Specialized high-performance or AI-heavy tiers.
- **Infra as a Service**: Automated provisioning and scaling.
- **Marketplace**: Reusable modules and prebuilt Temps.

## Roles & Permissions
- **Igniter (Client)**: Rent Temps, view progress in real-time ("Tent Status"), and own the final product.
- **Builder (Creator)**: Connect to the Temp, execute on the SoT, and get paid for contributions.
- **Guardian (Admin)**: Oversee ecosystem quality, curate the Temp registry, and manage the marketplace.
- **Brain (AI Assistant)**: Analyze SoT, suggest enhancements, and maintain decision history.
